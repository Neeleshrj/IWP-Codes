Jungle Combing Operation,Chambal Valley

In 2018,I went with my father to the Chambal Valley region to hunt some Bandits.The area has been infested with bandits for over 20years.So we went there to comb the area for bandits.

We wore Jungle camo and there were 20 people.Everyone carried Assualt Rifles.We patrolled a route and walked 14km on foot.There was also a climb of 6km.

By the end of the day,eveyone was tired and we went back to the forward operating camp.
