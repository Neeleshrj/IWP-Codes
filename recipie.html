Ingredients

  • 1 can(400g) of chick peas
  • 175g of tahini
  • 6 sundried tomatoes
  • Half red pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • A dash of olive oil

Instructions

  1. Remove skin from garlic
  2. Remove all the seeds and stalk from pepper and chop
  3. Add all Ingredients into food processor
  4. Process all Ingredients into a paste

For a different flavour,you could try blending in a small measure of lemon and coriander,chilli pepper,lime and chipotle
